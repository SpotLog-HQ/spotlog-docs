# My Spots

My Spots is where you can find all of your spots that you have recorded within SpotLog

<a href="/public/images/getting-started/my-spots.png" target="_blank">
  <img
    src="/public/images/getting-started/my-spots.png"
    class="doc-img md"
    alt="SpotLog – My Spots screen"
  />
</a>

It is possible to [filter](/my-spots/filtering) these spots, this is explained in more detail on the [filtering](/my-spots/filtering) page.

## Chronological Navigation

My Spots contains a chronological navigation bar down the right side of the screen, this allows you to navigate through your spots by Year or Month, you can click on a month within the chronological list and you will be taken to that month.

<a href="/public/images/getting-started/my-spots-chronological.png" target="_blank">
  <img
    src="/public/images/getting-started/my-spots-chronological.png"
    class="doc-img md"
    alt="SpotLog – My Spots screen"
  />
</a>

It is possible to scroll the chronological list to get more years or months to appear if you have a extensive record of spots.
