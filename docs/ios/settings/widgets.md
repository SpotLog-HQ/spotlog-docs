# Home Screen Widgets

SpotLog offers home screen widgets that let you quickly access app features or see your spotting information at a glance, without opening the full app.

<!-- Screenshot: The Widgets information screen showing the widget carousel -->

## Available Widgets

### Most Recent Spot

Shows your latest logged spot directly on your home screen. At a glance, you can see:

- The locomotive number and name
- Location where you spotted it
- Time of the sighting
- A photo (if attached)

<!-- Screenshot: The Most Recent Spot widget preview -->

### Quick Log

A simple shortcut widget that opens the Quick Log screen directly. Perfect for when you're in a hurry and want to record a spot as fast as possible.

<!-- Screenshot: The Quick Log widget preview -->

### SpotLog Stats

Displays key statistics from your spotting activity:

- Total spots recorded
- Spots this month
- Your most spotted locomotive class

<!-- Screenshot: The Stats widget preview -->

## Adding Widgets to Your Home Screen

### iOS

1. Long-press on your home screen until apps start to jiggle
2. Tap the **+** button in the top corner
3. Search for "SpotLog"
4. Choose your preferred widget size
5. Tap **Add Widget**
6. Position the widget where you want it

<!-- Screenshot: iOS widget gallery showing SpotLog widgets -->

### Android

1. Long-press on an empty area of your home screen
2. Select **Widgets** from the menu
3. Find "SpotLog" in the widget list
4. Long-press the widget you want
5. Drag it to your home screen

<!-- Screenshot: Android widget picker showing SpotLog widgets -->

## Widget Sizes

Some widgets are available in different sizes:

- **Small** - Compact, shows essential info
- **Medium** - More detail, good balance of size and information
- **Large** - Full-featured with maximum information

The available sizes depend on your device and the specific widget.

## Widget Updates

Widgets update automatically to show your latest information. The Most Recent Spot widget, for example, will update whenever you log a new spot.

## Tips

- Add the Quick Log widget for fastest access when spotting
- Use the Stats widget to stay motivated with your spotting goals
- The Most Recent Spot widget is great for remembering what you last saw
