# My Spots

My Spots is your complete collection of recorded sightings. Here you can browse, manage, and view details for all the spots you've logged.

## Spot List

Spots are displayed in chronological order with the most recent at the top. Each spot in the list shows:

- Locomotive number and name
- Date spotted
- Location (if recorded)
- A photo indicator if photos are attached

Tap any spot to view its full [Spot Details](spot-details).

## Managing Your Spots

From the My Spots list, you can:

- **View details** - Tap a spot to see all recorded information
- **Edit** - Swipe left and tap Edit to modify a spot
- **Delete** - Swipe left and tap Delete to remove a spot
- **Filter** - Use the filter panel to find specific spots

## Swipe Actions

SpotLog uses swipe gestures to reveal action buttons:

1. **Swipe left** on any spot to reveal the action buttons
2. You'll see **Edit** and **Delete** buttons
3. Tap the action you want to perform
4. Tap elsewhere or swipe back to close the actions
