# Detailed Log - Photos

This section allows you to add photos to your spot.

<a href="/images/detailed-log/detailed-log-other.png" target="_blank">
  <img

</a>

Using the camera button within SpotLog your phone gallery will open where you can select your photos

<a href="/images/detailed-log/detailed-log-add-photos-1.png" target="_blank">
  <img
    src="/images/detailed-log/detailed-log-add-photos-1.png"

</a>

Once you have selected the photos that you wish to add

<a href="/images/detailed-log/detailed-log-add-photos-2.png" target="_blank">

    src="/images/detailed-log/detailed-log-add-photos-2.png"
    class="doc-img md"
    alt="SpotLog – Calendar screen"
  />
</a>

You can save your spot and the photos will automatically be saved.

<a href="/images/detailed-log/detailed-log-add-photos-3.png" target="_blank">
  <img
    src="/images/detailed-log/detailed-log-add-photos-3.png"
    class="doc-img md"

All your photos can be viewed in the [SpotLog gallery](/getting-started/gallery)
