## Welcome to SpotLog Support

SpotLog is the train spotting companion app designed for UK rail enthusiasts. Whether you're logging quick sightings or recording detailed information with photos, SpotLog helps you keep track of every locomotive you see.

### Quick Links

- [Quick Log](/ios/getting-started/quick-log) - Log a spot in seconds
- [Stats](/ios/getting-started/stats) - See your spotting statistics
- [Import/Export](/ios/settings/import-export/import) - Backup and restore your data
- [Widgets](/ios/settings/widgets) - Add SpotLog to your home screen

## Need Help?

If you can't find what you're looking for in this documentation, [contact us](mailto:support@spotlog.app) and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
